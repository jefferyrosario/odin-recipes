Philly Cheese Steak

The philly cheese steak is a treasure of Philadelphia. Have a look at the sandwich:

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Steak
  • Peppers
  • Onions

Steps

  • Cut the bread open
  • Cook the steak
  • Make sure you are cooking the peppers and onions with the steak
  • Once cooked, add the steak on the bread
  • Add the cheese to make a nice melt
